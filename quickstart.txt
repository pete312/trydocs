The demo uses Python virtual envrionments and this can be activated and deactivated with .. 

    source <path to env>/bin/activate

.. and to deactivate again just enter the command deactivate. Once activated you will have mkdocs utility in your PATH


To start the demo 

    mkdocs serve 

and open a browser with the url http:/127.0.0.1:8000/


To make your own stie

    mkdocs new <dir for your site>
    cd <dir for your site>
    mkdocs serve

So to get started just enable your env with ...
